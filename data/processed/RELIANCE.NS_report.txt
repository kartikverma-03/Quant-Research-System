**Reliance Industries Limited (RELIANCE.NS) - Equity Research Report**

**Business Overview:** Reliance Industries is a diversified Indian conglomerate with extensive interests spanning energy, retail, and digital services.

**Valuation Assessment:** Trading at ₹1354.5, RELIANCE's P/E of 22.68x and P/B of 2.03x suggest a fair valuation. The stock is currently ~16% below its 52-week high of ₹1611.8, offering some potential entry buffer.

**Financial Health & Profitability:** The company exhibits a Profit Margin of 7.64% and Return on Equity of 9.14%. Critically, an exceptionally high Debt/Equity ratio of 36.65x raises significant concerns regarding financial leverage and long-term stability.

**Key Risks:** Primary risks include extreme leverage (D/E 36.65x), volatility in commodity prices impacting its energy segment, and intense competition across its retail and telecom verticals. Regulatory changes also pose risks.

**Verdict:** **HOLD.** While Reliance possesses strong growth engines in retail and digital, the alarmingly high Debt/Equity ratio (36.65x) presents a material overhang. We recommend a Hold rating, pending clear de-leveraging strategies.
