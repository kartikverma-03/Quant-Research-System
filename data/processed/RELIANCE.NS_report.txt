**Reliance Industries Ltd. (RELIANCE.NS) – Equity Research Report**

**Business Overview:** Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) is a diversified Indian conglomerate with extensive interests in energy (refining, petrochemicals), retail, telecom (Jio Platforms), and digital services.

**Valuation & Financials:** Trading near its 52-week low of 1290.0 at 1321.2, RELIANCE's P/E of 22.13 and Price/Book of 1.98 suggest a premium valuation, likely reflecting its growth segments. Financial health indicates a decent Profit Margin of 7.64%, but a low Return on Equity (ROE) of 9.14% and a critically high Debt/Equity ratio of 36.65 point to significant leverage and potential efficiency challenges.

**Key Risks:** Major risks include substantial debt servicing costs, volatility in crude oil prices, intense competition in its consumer-facing businesses, and evolving regulatory landscapes across its diverse operations.

**Verdict: Hold.** While RIL possesses robust growth avenues in its consumer and digital businesses, the exceptionally high Debt/Equity ratio and modest ROE introduce considerable risk. We recommend investors monitor deleveraging efforts and sustained margin improvement closely before considering further investment.
